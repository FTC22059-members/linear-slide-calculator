In Order to Reach the Target

The Retracted Step is the offset between the bottoms of two adjacent bars when the linear slide is retracted.

The Extended Step is the offset between the bottoms of two adjacent bars when the linear slide is extended.

The Target Retracted Height is height that the linear slide should be from the floor to the top when it is retracted

The Target Extended Height is height that the linear slide should be from the floor to the tippity top when it is extended.

The Number of Segments is the number of segments in the linear slide.

The Bar Length is the length of (usually) the 80/20, 20/20, 15/15, etc.

The Chasis Height is distance from the floor to the top of the chasis (of whatever the linear slide is attached to).

The Tension Drop is the percentage drop in height per segment due to the lack of perfect string tension.
